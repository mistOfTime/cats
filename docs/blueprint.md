# **App Name**: CIT-U SafeSpace

## Core Features:

- Anonymous Post Creation: Allow authenticated CIT-U students to submit anonymous text posts to a community feed for safe expression.
- Post Interaction: Enable users to react to posts with predefined emotions (e.g., Support, Relate, Encourage) and flag inappropriate content for moderation.
- Stress Level Assessment Tool: An interactive questionnaire to assess a user's current stress level, classifying it as Low, Moderate, or High.
- Personalized Stress Guidance: Based on stress assessment results, a generative AI tool provides automated, personalized recommendations for coping strategies or actions.
- Stress Assessment History: Allow users to review their past stress assessment results and observe trends over time.
- Curated Resource Library: Provide a categorized list of mental health hotlines, school services, and self-help tips, dynamically suggesting relevant resources based on stress levels.
- Admin Content Moderation: An administrative interface to review and manage flagged posts, ensure content adheres to community guidelines, and monitor keyword-based safety alerts through AI filtering.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color palette revolves around deep maroon (#800000) and rich gold (#FFD700), evoking a sense of strength, tradition, and warmth.
- The background color is a very dark, desaturated maroon (#2A0000) to maintain a private and calming ambiance while reducing eye strain.
- Gold (#FFD700) is used as a secondary color for prominent text, interactive elements, and important highlights, providing elegant contrast.
- A brighter accent gold (#FFC107) is utilized for calls to action, active states, and small, vibrant details to draw attention.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, slightly tech-inspired, and approachable feel in titles and headers.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its readability and neutrality in longer text passages, resource descriptions, and post content.
- Utilize simple, outline-style icons with rounded edges for a soft, approachable feel, complementing the focus on mental well-being and clear communication without harshness.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout for posts and resources, ensuring a visually organized and easily digestible interface. Prioritize responsiveness to maintain optimal usability and access across all desktop and mobile device sizes.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transitions and hover effects to provide interactive feedback and enhance user engagement without distracting from the sensitive nature of the application's content.
